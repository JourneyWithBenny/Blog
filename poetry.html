Crew House

The crew house, a place of rest when work is done it is shared by many, sometimes by none This place can be quiet, with no sounds to hear or booming with laughter and joyful tears How many people, have stayed in this spot When we are sent on rotation, and travel lots I look out the window, into the backyard watching the rope swing sway, near and far If this house could talk, I'm sure it would have stories to say, both good and bad, and in different ways so whether you're stay is long or fast the crew house remains, and will always last
